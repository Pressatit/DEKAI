##  DeKUT ANTHEM 
**The University Anthem**  
---  
**English Version**  
To God of Patriots we pray Guide our varsity Dedan Kimathi Excelling in research, innovation, education To foster better life through technology  
DeKUT, University of blessings The pride on the slopes of Mount KenyaOur heritage, diverse cultures are our hallmark Glory and honor be to you oh God  
Home of heroes and talents harnessing Champion in holistic education Great goals we achieve through teamwork Oh Almighty God bless Kimathi
