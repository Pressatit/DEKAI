##  Pensions Office 
**Pensions Office Mandate**
To provide retirement and other benefits for employees of the Sponsor and the participating employees or the members’ respective dependants.
**Pensions Office Mission**
To provide a dignified lifestyle after retirement to members of DeKUT SRBS and the beneficiaries by maximising investment returns through the professional management of the Scheme.
**Pensions Office Vision**
A world-class pension scheme providing distinct quality life and financial security in retirement to its members and beneficiaries.
**Pensions Office Core Values**
A world-class pension scheme providing distinct quality life and financial security in retirement to its members and beneficiaries.
**Integrity:** Acting morally, honestly and legally
**Respect:** Recognising the worth, uniqueness of all stakeholders
**Collaboration:** Working as one with all stakeholders
**Accountability:** Doing the right thing and owning the outcomes of our actions for the benefit of the members
**Excellence:** Doing our very best
**Stewardship:** Prudent investments and timely disbursement of benefits
**Trust:** Executing of fiduciary responsibilities by the Board of Trustees or the benefit of the members
**Professionalism:** Compliance with the rules, regulations and the industry practices
