# Institute of Technical and Professional Studies (ITPS)
The abbreviation of Institute of Technical and Professional Studies is ITPS
The director for Institute of Technical and Professional Studies is Dr. Erastus Karoki BEd-BT (Moi), MSc Civil (Wuhan Univ. of Tech.), PhD Civil (Shanghai JiaoTong University)

Welcome to ITPS; As Kenya strives to be a middle income economy, Technical and Professional training is critical to enable her acquire the necessary human resource base. High quality training services must be provided to Kenyans to propel realization of vision 2030.
Pursue a Certificate or Diploma course with us with a promise of timely completion, career progression, quality and holistic training ensuring job placement and creation, serene learning environment among others.
To achieve this, DeKUT under ITPS offers:
  1. Professional courses that are set examined and certified by recognized National/ International professional bodies.
  2. Technical, Industrial and Vocational diploma and certificate courses that addresses skill needs in various sectors of the Kenyan economy. As much as possible the courses are structured to allow for credit transfers to degree programs. The diploma and certificate courses are developed in conjunction with appropriate Schools and/or Centers.
  3. Vocational certificate courses including crafts and trade tests.
  4. Short competency based courses that improve on trainees’ skills and abilities to work in various sectors of the economy.


Dedan Kimathi University of Technology (DeKUT) therefore presents you an opportunity for world class Technical and Professional training.


# About the Institute of Technical and Professional Studies 
**Institute of Technical and Professional Studies Vision**
To be a house hold name in Technical, Vocational and Professional training, both locally and beyond, whereby students achieve their potential for growth and success by providing quality training programs and innovative instruction.

**Institute of Technical and Professional Studies Mission**
To provide Technical, vocational and professional training which is responsive to the real and current needs of Kenya and thereby apply research, innovation and technology to produce confident technical professionals for leadership in Job creation and attainment of national goals and Vision 2030.[Skip to content](https://itps.dkut.ac.ke/programmes/#content)
Dedan Kimathi University of Technology invites qualified form four leavers to apply for admission at the Main Campus or Nairobi Campus.
