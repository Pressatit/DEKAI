# Programmes offered by Institute of Criminology, Forensics and Security Studies (ICFoSS)
**1. Bachelor of Science in Criminology and Security Management** The Bachelors Degree in Criminology and Security Management takes 8 Semesters, and there are new entrants each semester. The program entry requirements are aligned according to CUE regulations. The program is designed for those students who are interested in applied criminology and security management.The program has three majors that are:-
**a) Security Management and Loss Prevention Technology** This specialization is designed for those students who are interested in security management and loss prevention. It is target to those students who want to pursue security management in the corporate sector. Those course offered in here focuses on: Physical Security, Business and People Protection, Asset and Personnel Security, CCTV and Access Control Technology, etc.
**b) Forensic Science and Criminal Investigation** This specialization is designed for those students who are interested in criminal investigation and forensic science. It is designed to advance skills in application of science and technology in criminal investigation. Course offered here include: Forensic Science and Technology, Forensic Psychology, Crime Scene Management, Finger Print Science, Fire and Explosives investigations, etc
**c) Cyber Crime and Digital Forensic Technology** This specialization is designed for those students who are interested in Cyber Crime and Digital Forensic Technology. It is designed to advance students skills in investigation of computer related crimes. Courses offered here include: Digital Forensics, Data Mining, Internet Technologies, Cyber and Computer Forensics, etc.
**2. Masters of Science in Forensics and Security Management** The Masters of Forensics and Security Management takes 2 Academic Years an equivalent of 4 Semesters, and there are new entrants each semester. The MSC program entry requirements are aligned according to CUE regulations. The MSC program is designed for those students who are interested in Corporate Forensic and Security Management.The program has three majors that are:-
**a) Corporate Forensics Management** This specialization is targeted at producing students interested in mastering Corporate Security. The course focuses on courses such as: Corporate Risk Management, Procurement Fraud, Financial Crime and Audit, Banking Fraud Investigation.**b) Security Management** This specialization is targeted at security managers, and focuses on: Risk Assessment, Intelligence Management, Regional and Global Security, Information Security Management, etc.**c) Cybercrime Forensics Management** This specialization is targeted at investigations of computer related crimes, mobile phones and wireless devices. This course on offer include: Digital Forensics, Cyber Intelligence, Cyber Warfare, Cyber Incident Analysis, etc.
