# About School of Computer Science and Information Technology
 
**Overview of the School of Computer Science and Information Technology**

The School of Computer Science and Information Technology (CSIT) produces high skilled graduates who play a significant role in the Kenyan and Global economy. The School seeks to significantly enhance and expand the visibility of Computing Technology innovative solutions to national and global audience. The School will provide the appropriate environment to ensure that the highest professional standards are achieved and maintained.

**School of Computer Science and Information Technology Vision**

To provide innovative and high quality computing and Informatics education at the doorsteps of learners with learner-centric approach exploiting the science of computing and information platforms.

**School of Computer Science and Information Technology Mission**

To provide access to higher educational opportunities in Computing Technology programmes that will enable learners to develop theoretical, practical, and ethical skills and knowledge necessary to achieve their professional goals, improve the productivity of their organizations and provide valuable and creative, innovative and resourceful contribution to society.

## BSc. Computer Science
# MAIN CAMPUS

**Bachelor of Science in Computer Science**
Minimum Entry Requirements

KCSE applicants: mean grade C+ and the following:
Alternative A: Mathematics C+, Physics C+, Chemistry C+
Alternative B: Mathematics C+, Physical Sciences C+ and Geography or any group IV subjects’ C+ or equivalent qualifications obtained in other examination systems.
OR
KACE or A-Level applicants: must have at least two principal passes in Mathematics and Physics and at least a credit pass in Chemistry at the KCE or be a holder of an equivalent qualification as recognized by the University Senate.
Mode of Study : Full time
Duration: 4 Years

## BSc. Information Technology
# MAIN CAMPUS
**Bachelor of Science in Information Technology**

Minimim Entry Requirements

KCSE Aggregate Grade of C+ (plus) and with a C (plain) in Mathematics and English
OR
KACE or A-Level applicants must have at least two principal passes or an equivalent qualification as recognized by DeKUT Senate
Mode of Study : Full Time& Evening
Duration: 8 Semesters offered in 3 years (Trimester system)

**Bachelor of Science in Information Technology (Module II)**
Minimim Entry Requirements

Be a holder of a Diploma in Information Technology from DeKUT with CREDIT pass or any other qualification recognized by DeKUT Senate.

Mode of Study:Full Time & Evening

Duration: 5 Semesters offered in 2 years (Trimester system)

## BSc. Business Information Technology
# MAIN CAMPUS

**Bachelor of Science in Business Information Technology**
Minimum Entry Requirements

KCSE Aggregate Grade of C+ (plus) and with C (plain) in Mathematics and English
OR
KACE with at least two principal passes and one Subsidiary and a minimum of a Credit Pass in Mathematics at KCE
OR
Be a holder of a Diploma or professional certificate in a relevant discipline from an institution recognized by DeKUT Senate

Mode of Study: Full Time
Duration: 8 Semesters offered in 3 years (Trimester system)
