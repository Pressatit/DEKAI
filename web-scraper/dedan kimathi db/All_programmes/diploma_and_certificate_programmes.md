##  Diploma and Certificate Programmes 
## Diploma in Sustainable Tourism & Hospitality Management
This **Two (2)** years Programme seeks to equip learners with technical knowledge, skills and competencies that will enable them to contribute effectively to the sustenance and growth of tourism and hospitality industry through the application of sustainability principles in operations, product development, packaging, marketing and customer satisfaction management. After training the trainee should be able to:
  1. Apply technical knowledge, skills and attitudes for effective development and management of sustainable tourism and hospitality ventures.
  2. Develop and produce different types of foods and beverages to the required standards of catering and accommodation establishment.
  3. Apply the principles of professional tour guiding and interpretation.
  4. Apply the principles of cost control in order to minimize cost and maximize profit.
  5. Demonstrate an understanding of the various components of tourism and hospitality sectors.
  6. Utilise, maintain and care for the tools and equipment used in tourism and hospitality establishment.
  7. Apply entrepreneurship, marketing and management skills to start and operate a tourism and hospitality venture.


****Diploma in Sustainable Tourism & Hospitality Management Course Requirements****
  1. Passed in relevant craft course;
  2. Passed Kenya Certificate of Secondary Education (KCSE) with an average grade of C- (C Minus); Or,
  3. A holder of any relevant qualifications from accredited institutions; Or,
  4. Equivalent qualifications as determined by Kenya National Examinations Council; Or,
  5. Relevant qualifications recognized by the National Qualification Framework Regulation.


## Diploma in Security Management
Diploma in Security Management course provides comprehensive training in order to enhance professionalism and skills in Public and private sector security management.
This diploma covers issues related to administration and management of security of enterprise information systems and networks.
The diploma is aimed at developing capabilities to design detailed enterprise wide security plans and policies, and deploy appropriate safeguards (models, mechanisms and tools) at all the levels by providing due consideration to the life-cycle of the enterprise information systems and networks, as well as its legal and social environment. 
****Course Requirements for Diploma in Security Management****
KCSE Mean Grade C-

#Diploma in Information Technology (KNQF Level 6)
Diploma in Information Technology prepares learners for autonomous ICT practice by developing competencies in system design, software development, databases, cybersecurity, data analytics, cloud technologies, and emerging ICT trends. The programme integrates theory, practical training, industrial attachment, and capstone innovation project.
**Duration**
Five (5) academic modules inclusive of industrial attachment and capstone project
**Course Requirements**
  1. A KCSE Mean Grade C- (minus) or equivalent qualification as recognized by DeKUT University Senate.**OR**
  2. A KNQF Level 5 Certificate in a related ICT discipline recognized by KNQA and the university senate**OR**
  3. An applicant with relevant ICT work experience, professional certifications or informal training through Recognition of Prior Learning KNQF Level 5, subject to assessment and approval in line with KNQA and TVET RPL guidelines.


**Diploma in Information Technology Career Opportunities**
Graduates may work as ICT Officers, System Administrators, software developers, database administrators, network technicians, cybersecurity assistants, ICT project assistants or pursue self-employment in ICT services.
**Progression Opportunities**
Graduates are eligible to progress to KNQF level 7 programmes, including Bachelor’s degrees in Information Technology, Computer Science, Software Engineering, or related fields, subject to meeting admission requirements of the respective School and programs.

## Certificate in Coffee Technology & Quality Management
Coffee cupping and technology is a course which trains its students in coffee cupping, or coffee tasting, is the practice of observing the tastes and aromas of brewed coffee. It is a professional practice but can be done informally by anyone or by professionals. This Certificate course gives you the skills and training necessary to become a professional in understanding basic tastes of coffee and where different coffees should be slotted into blends.
**Course Requirements**
Applicants should have obtained a minimum of mean of D+ (Plus) in Kenya Certificate of Secondary Education (KCSE) or equivalent. Besides the minimum requirements, applicants shall have passed in specific subjects as follows: Biology D, Chemistry D, Mathematics D, Physical and Biological Sciences D. Applicants with D with relevant coffee certificate training OR one year experience in the coffee industry will be considered subject to approval by the DeKUT Senate.
