##  Undergraduate Programmes 

# Bachelor of Business Administration
Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA) programme aims to provide the students with skills and enhance self confidence and ability to evaluate administration issues from academic and practical perspective. The course prepares students for a variety of different management and administrative roles It is designed to help students learn to take on an executive role in planning, organizing, staffing, directing and controlling business operations. At DeKUT, Business Administration enables students to gain a clear understanding of various business disciplines to major in at their third year of studies, i.e. Marketing Accounting, Entrepreneurship, Leadership & Management, and Strategic Management Option, Human Resource Management, International Business Management, Finance Management & Investment Banking.
**Bachelor of Business Administration Requirements**
Entry requirements are at least a mean grade of C+ (plus) with a C (plain) in Mathematics and English or Kiswahili.

# Bachelor of Science in Geology
The Undergraduate Bachelor of Science degree in Geology is a 4 year program that is designed to both prepare students into the workforce as practicing geoscientists, and to provide them with knowledge required to pursue an advance degree. Students enrolled in Geology program at DeKUT are afforded the opportunity to pursue interests in several areas as specialization including petrology, volcanology, structural geology, sedimentary geology, climate change and soil science.
This program helps students learn about the composition of Earth and it's atmosphere; how natural forces, such as wind and water, shape land; and how to analyze Earth's layers, rock and mineral formations and raw materials, like oil and water. # Bachelor of Science in Geospatial Information Science & Remote Sensing
This four year Bachelor of Science in Geospatial Information Science and Remote Sensing is designed for students who desire to find employment in the private and public sectors, particularly within the fields such as environment, urban planning, or disaster management. Taken on campus at The Dedan Kimathi University of Technology, this programme takes place over two semesters, with a research project in the final year to give students a comprehensive and well-rounded learning experience.
