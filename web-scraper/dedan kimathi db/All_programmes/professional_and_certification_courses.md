# Cisco Certified Network Associate (CCNA)
**What is CCNA?**
  * CCNA stands for Cisco Certified Network Associate. It is a networking certification offered by Cisco, the world's leading networking equipment manufacturer.
  * The CCNA is a valuable asset for anyone looking to start a career in IT networking. It can open doors to jobs such as network technician, network administrator, cybersecurity specialCisco Systems, a leading provider of networking and telecommunications equipment and services, offer the CCNA certification and troubleshoot local area networks (LANs).

**How is Cisco Certified Network Associate (CCNA) structured?**
  * It is a three-module series:
    * Module 1: Introduction to Networks
    * Module 2: Switching, Routing and Wireless Essentials
    * Module 3: Enterprise Networking, Security and Automation

**What are the benefits of CCNA?**
  * **_Enhanced Knowledge and Skills_** : You'll gain a solid understanding of fundamental networking concepts, technologies, and protocols used in modern networks, including routing, switching, IP addressing, and network security.
  * **_Improved Job Prospects_** : The CCNA certification is globally recognised and highly sought-after by employers.
  * **_Global Recognition_** : The CCNA certification is recognised worldwide, opening up career opportunities in various countries.
  * **_Strong Foundation for Further Certifications_** : The CCNA is a prerequisite for several advanced Cisco certifications, allowing you to specialise in specific networking areas like cybersecurity or cloud networking.

**What is the entry requirement for CCNA?**
  * All registered DeKUT students enrolled in any diploma or undergraduate course are eligible.
  * A student needs to have completed KCSE enrol for the course.

**What is the mode of learning for CCNA?**
  * The course is **online** every weekday, Tuesday to Thursday, 5.30 to 6.30 PM.
  * A student must have access to a laptop/PC and a reliable internet connection to attend class sessions and access course materials, practical assessments and examinations.

**What is the cost of the Cisco Certified Network Associate (CCNA) programme?**
  * KES 18,000 – DeKUT students
  * KES 22,500 – Non-DeKUT students

## Masterly in ArchiCAD: Advanced BIM(Building Information Modelling)
This is a structured competency short course designed to equip learners with technical skills in 2D drafting, 3D modeling, and Building Information Modeling (BIM) using ArchiCAD. The program progress through three levels i.e. Essentials, Intermediate and Advanced focusing on real projects design application in construction documentation, visualization and collaborative workflows with industry standards.
The program is ideal for students, professionals, and interested self-driven learners. The program combines instructor led training, hands-on projects and performance based assessments to ensure job-ready proficiency in digital architectural design.
**Masterly in ArchiCAD Requirements**
  1. Minimum of a Certificate level in Architecture or Engineering or related fields and basic knowledge in computer skills
OR
  2. KCSE certificate and basic computer skills
OR
  3. Interested parties with informal training and has basic computer skills


## Mountaineering and Outdoor Expeditions Guiding
This three weeks short course programme seeks to equip trainees with appropriate attitude, practical and theoretical concepts, knowledge and competencies needed to professionally and successfully execute mountaineering and outdoor expeditions.
By the end of three weeks intensive training the trainee should be able to:
  1. Confidently initiate and guide mountaineering and outdoor expedition;
  2. Evaluate, reduce and avoid hazards;
  3. Professionally guide tourists/guests and market tour guiding services;
  4. Provide basic Wilderness First Aid in the event of an emergence during mountaineering and outdoor activities expedition;
  5. Promote responsible mountaineering and outdoor activities;
  6. Plan, implement and evaluate mountaineering and outdoor activities.


**Mountaineering and Outdoor Expeditions Guiding Course Requirements**
  1. Be a holder of KCSE certificate with a minimum aggregate of D- and a minimum of D- in English and/ or Kiswahili. **Or**
  2. Any person who is engaged in mountaineering and expedition guiding (this includes the expedition crew) ready and comfortable to learn in English.
