## Our Courses
CODeL does not have its own programmes. In DeKUT, students and programmes belong to departments. The center facilitates offering all the programmes approved by the DeKUT senate and accredited by CUE and other relevant accreditation bodies. CODeL provides the required platforms, lecturer training and technical support for online teaching
By leveraging modern e-learning technologies, the center facilitates blended learning approach that combines online resources, interactive platforms, and occasional face-to-face sessions, ensuring a comprehensive learning experience. 
### [SCHOOL OF ENGINEERING](https://soe.dkut.ac.ke/)
Engineering is the creative application of science, mathematical methods, and empirical evidence to the innovation, design, construction, and maintenance of structures, machines, materials, devices, systems, processes, and organizations.
### [SCHOOL OF BUSINESS MANAGEMENT AND ECONOMICS](https://sbme.dkut.ac.ke/)
The Bachelor of Business and Commerce is a professionally-oriented degree program. The aim of the program is to produce graduates who are equipped to identify, describe, analyse and resolve business problems in both the public and private sectors. 
### [SCHOOL OF COMPUTER SCIENCE AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY](https://csit.dkut.ac.ke/)
At the School of Computer Science and Information Technology, programs have been qualitatively packaged to give our students the necessary skills to enable them compete favorably in the Job Market. The University has partnered and collaborated with renowned Technology Industry players. 
### [INSTITUTE OF TECHNICAL AND PROFESSIONAL STUDIES (ITPS)](https://itps.dkut.ac.ke/)
ITPS provides strategic IT consultancy, implementation, data centre services and unified communications, as well as support services and workspace and disaster recovery..
### [INSTITUTE OF TOURISM AND HOSPITALITY MANAGEMENT](https://itohm.dkut.ac.ke/)
Tourism and hospitality industries are among the world’s fastest growing economic sectors and important drivers for socio-economic and environmental progress. The industries enhance globalization through international commerce that generate foreign incomes for developing nations and create employment opportunities across the globe.
### [INSTITUTE OF FOOD AND BIORESOURCE TECHNOLOGY](http://ifbt.dkut.ac.ke/)
The following programmes are offered by the Institute: (i) M.Sc. degree in Food Science and Technology, (ii) B.Sc. degree in Food Science and Technology, (iii) B.Sc. degree in Nutrition and Dietetics, (iv) Certificate/Diploma in Coffee Technology and Quality Management.
