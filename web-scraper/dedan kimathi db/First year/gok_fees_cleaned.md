## Please use the following procedure during fees payment:
1. On your browser, type and open the link https://dekutservices.dkut.ac.ke/to access the application for DeKUT Services
2. Click make payments for student
3.Upon successful filling of the student details i.e. Phone Number, Registration Number, Email, ID Number, Amount
4. Select Make Payment
5. You wil be directed to DeKUT Services Page where there is "PAYMENT REF" and "Select Payment Mode".
6. Copy the PAYMENT REF
7. Go to your phone M-PESA tool kit or app and follow the instructions below:
a) Go to MPESA menu on your phone
b) Select Pay bill option
c) Enter the Business Number 222222
d) Enter or paste the PAYMENT REF (see number 5 above)
e) Enter the amount invoiced (TOTAL BILL)
REGISTRAR (AA & R)
f) Enter your MPESA PIN and Send
g) You wil receive a confirmation SMS from MPESA
h) Normal M-Pesa charges apply
